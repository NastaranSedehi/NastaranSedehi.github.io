About Me

Hello! Welcome to my homepage! I got My BSc in Software Engineering from the University of Isfahan, one of the best universities in Iran, specially in Computer Engineering. I was passionate about computer science and programming since high school and to pursue my passion I participated in different Robotics competitions. My team and I became the second best team in IranOpen RoboCup competitions in Junior Soccer league two years in a row. Later, in university I grew a specific interest in Artificial Intelligence. I did research in different subfields of it including Machine Learning, Deep Learning, data mining, and Computer Vision. It's safe to say that I am passionate about any part of the computer world where you can get exceptional results or help robots to get smarter and interact with people better by analyzing data ! Scroll down for more information about my academic journey. Thanks for Visiting!
